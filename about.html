Poseidon's Domain

Poseidon's domain extended over all bodies of water, including seas, rivers, and lakes. Sailors and fishermen worshiped him for protection and safe passage. Poseidon's influence was not limited to the sea and he was also revered as the creator of horses and associated with chariot racing.

Worship and Influence

Poseidon was widely worshiped across ancient Greece, particularly in coastal cities and islands. Temples dedicated to him, such as the famous Temple of Poseidon at Cape Sounion, were often located near the sea, where his power was most evident.
