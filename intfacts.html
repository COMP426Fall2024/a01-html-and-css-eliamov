Here are some lesser-known facts about Poseidon:

  • Poseidon was known to have a temper, which often resulted in violent storms and earthquakes.
  • He created the first horse and gifted it to humans.
  • His trident was a symbol of his power and was believed to have the ability to shake the earth and shatter any object.
  • Poseidon had many children which includes the hero Theseus and the giant Antaeus.
